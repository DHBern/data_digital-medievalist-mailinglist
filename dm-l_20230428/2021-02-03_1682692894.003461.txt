Subject: [dm-l] Save the date: Helsinki Digital Humanities Hackathon #DHH21

Helsinki Digital Humanities Hackathon #DHH21 19.–28.5.2021
http://heldig.fi/dhh21

It’s comin’ back around again! Helsinki Digital Humanities Hackathon
#DHH21 will be organised 19.-28.5.2021 as CLARIN and DARIAH
international summer school with NewsEye project etc. involved. The
event will be organized as an online hackathon.

Participation to #DHH21 is free to all accepted participants.

5 ECTS credits may be gained from participating in the hackathon for
students in University of Helsinki and other universities.

Ap­plic­a­tion sched­ule for #DH­H21:

* 9.–31.3.2021 Application period
* 9.4.2021 Applicants informed of acceptance
* 9.–14.4.2021 Registration to #DHH21 for accepted participants
* 19.–28.5.2021 #DHH21 hackathon online

Information about hackathon themes, data and team leaders will follow.

Save the date and spread the word!
