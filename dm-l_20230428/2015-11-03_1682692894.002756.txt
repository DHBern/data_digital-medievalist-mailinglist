*Digital modelling workshop for humanities researchers and educators*

Day workshop on digital modelling for beginners.

Monday 14th Dec, University of Reading, 10.00-17.00.

http://bit.ly/1MTgx2h

The day will introduce humanities researchers and teachers to the basics
of 3D modelling in SketchUp <http://www.sketchup.com>, and show some of
the uses to which these techniques can be put.

The day is led by Dr Matthew Nicholls, whose work
<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oCWWnVvpUfY> on digital modelling of
Roman sites won the 2014 Guardian/HEA Teaching Excellence Award
<http://www.theguardian.com/higher-education-network/2014/feb/18/winner-university-of-reading-teaching-excellence>. 



The workshop is generously funded by the British Academy’s ‘Rising Star
Engagement Award’ grant scheme
<https://www.timeshighereducation.com/news/british-academy-announces-rising-star-engagement-winners/2019375.article>. 



Free of charge; all welcome. Some financial assistance with travel costs 
may be available to students wishing to attend.
