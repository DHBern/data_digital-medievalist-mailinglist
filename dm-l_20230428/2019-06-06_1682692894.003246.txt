This is a final reminder to submit nominations to the DM 2019-2020 Board. The deadline is this Saturday, June 8.



Digital Medievalist will be holding elections at the end of June 2019 for five positions to its Executive Board. Board positions are for two year terms and incumbents may be re-elected (for a maximum of three terms in a row). Members of the Board are responsible for the overall direction of the organisation and leading the Digital Medievalistâ€™s many projects and programmes. For further information about the Executive Board and Digital Medievalist more generally, please see the DM website, particularly:
