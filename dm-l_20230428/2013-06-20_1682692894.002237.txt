- Social networks: you do have a point  about the segmentation; I use Facebook a lot for both professional and personal contacts, and I have an academia.edu<http://academia.edu/> account, but I don't use it very actively and do not wish to have to follow news and posts on several networks.

- Twitter: not good for academic communities IMHO.
