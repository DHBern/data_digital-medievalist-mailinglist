Subject: [dm-l] Fwd: Summer School: Mss Description and Edition of MSS

Dear all,

I am glad to announce that a summer school is organized in Saint-Omer
(France) from Aug. 25th to Aug. 29th, 2015: *Reconstruct a medieval library
today: Saint-Bertin *(Reconstituer une bibliothèque médiévale aujourd'hui.
L'exemple de Saint-Bertin).

The funding of Biblissima <http://biblissima-condorcet.fr/>, IRHT
<http://www.irht.cnrs.fr>, Saint-Omer
<http://www.bibliotheque-agglo-stomer.fr/> allows it to be free of charge
for participants (accomodation and meals included, no course fees).
Students will learn how to describe MSS (scientific description and TEI
encoding), how to scholarly edit inventories of MSS collections and how to
interconnect metadata from MSS descriptions and inventories to digitized
items, in order to better understand book production and use in the Middle
Ages and in the early modern period.

Papers will be given in French. Students have to present a poster.

Application until June 9th, 2015 ( application form)
<http://www.irht.cnrs.fr/sites/default/files/images/images_contenu/images_contenu_site/pieces_jointes/formulaire_dinscription_saint-omer.pdf>.
Please circulate this information.

Best regards,
Dominique Stutzmann

Program

*Mardi 25 août 2015 – L’histoire des bibliothèques médiévales*

   - 9h-9h30 – Accueil
   - 9h30-12h30 – Anne-Marie TURCAN-VERKERK, Les collections de livres du Ve

   - 9h-12h30 – Laura LEBARBEY, Reconstituer et étudier une collection
