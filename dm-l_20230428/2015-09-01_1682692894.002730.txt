CALL FOR PAPERS
ALBERTA CONFERENCE ON LINGUISTICS (ACOL)
Saturday October 31, 2015
11:00-18:00
University of Lethbridge
Anderson Hall 116-117

Please send your abstract by Monday, September 28th, 2015 to Madoka
_redacted_(a)uleth.ca. Abstracts should be approximately 150
words exclusive of references and should be anonymous. Send your abstract
as a Word or Pdf file attachment and put your full name, rank, and contact
information in the body of your email. We welcome proposals for papers in
any area of linguistics. Students are especially encouraged to submit. Talk
length (15 or 20 minutes + 5 minutes for questions) will be determined once
we know the number of papers.

As always, there is no registration fee for this event. Morning coffee and
a light lunch are included. Please let us know if you have any dietary
restrictions.
We are planning an informal social event after the talks on Saturday night.

Dates:
September 28: abstract submission deadline.
October 9: notifications of acceptance sent out.
October 16: program sent out.
October 31: ACOL.
