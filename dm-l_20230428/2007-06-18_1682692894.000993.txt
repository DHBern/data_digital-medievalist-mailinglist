Subject: [dm-l] [Fwd: announcing the TEI Members Meeting, 31st October - 3rd

Let me recommend this meeting/conference. 

The format is quite different from previous years and will involve quite
an interesting conference in addition to the usual posters, keynotes,
and member's business. There is one session on the markup of
manuscripts, for example.

Also, we will be having a granting roundtable with representatives of
many of the major funding agencies: IMLS, NEH, NSF (U.S.), DFG
(Germany), almost certainly SSHRC (Canada) and possibly agencies from
the U.K. and EU. These will be discussing new directions in Digital
Humanities funding.

This should be a very interesting event.
