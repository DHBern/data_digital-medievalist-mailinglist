Subject: [dm-l] Web-based App to catalog an author's works

Dear DM-ers:

Does anyone know of a web-based app that assists in cataloguing the corpus
of an individual author?  I am thinking of something that organizes
according titles of works, along with listings of relevant MSS copies or
printed editions, permits adding bibliography for each work and even might
allow users to attach e-texts where available?

Many thanks
Jim
