Dear colleagues,A new project at Hamburg University aimed at developing a  
comprehensive research environment for Ethiopic text and manuscript  
studies is urgently looking for a Research Fellow in Digital  
Humanities / IT Developer to help us jump-start our research efforts  
by creating and managing the relational database suitable to the  
project needs.The position is full-time at German E13 tariff level, limited for the  
moment to three years. Experience in similar data management project  
and acquiaintance with TEI XML as well as with semantic web  
technologies is expected.The application deadline is 20 January 2016, and the job can start as  
soon as possible, preferably on or before 1 March 2016. Please see  
http://www1.uni-hamburg.de/ethiostudies/adw.html for more details.Please feel free to forward this message to those who you feel may be  
interested.Thank you and best regards
