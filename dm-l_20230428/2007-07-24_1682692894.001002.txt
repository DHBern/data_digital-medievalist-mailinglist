Subject: [dm-l] Junicode for Mac

I've just added a Macintosh-specific version of Junicode 0.6.15. It  
is functionally the same as Junicode for Windows/Linux, but perhaps a  
little easier to install, and tested in Mellel and XeTeX on the  
gorgeous MacBook that my department just bought for me. You must have  
OS X 10.4 ("Tiger")  or later if you want to see the pretty OpenType  
things.

http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id8636

Peter
