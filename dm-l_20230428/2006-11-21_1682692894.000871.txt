Subject: [dm-l] rethinking the future for Anastasia

Dear everyone
as many of you know: we have for some years been developing our own  
piece of XML software, rather outside the XML mainstream, as it  
happens: the blessed Anastasia.  She has done some wonderful things  
for us, but lately we have been thinking hard about where we go with  
her. Being outside the mainstream is never a particularly nice place  
to be -- and has thrown a very large burden on us, as we have not  
been able to use many of the wonderful tools that there are out there.
So, we have been thinking...
You can follow the process at
www.sd-editions.com/blog
Comments welcome!
