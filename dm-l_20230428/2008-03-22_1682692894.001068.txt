go to http://demo.iupr.org/layout/layout.php
and feed in your favourite manuscript page.  This is Google's  
'ocropus' software, the latest wonder from Google.  This demo shows  
the system trying to find the text on the page- that is, identify the  
lines and columns, etc.  It does not do too badly at that.  We have  
also played with Gamera, for the same purpose: http:// 
ldp.library.jhu.edu/projects/gamera/
all the best
