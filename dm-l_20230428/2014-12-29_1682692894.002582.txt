Dear Daniel,

I am not sure if this is the kind of input you'd like, but I use Google 
Drive and Google Docs with teams frequently.  They have proven to be 
invaluable tools.  I am not sure what software you are using to edit 
your documents, but Google Docs records history of every single change 
and you can switch between "Edit" and "Suggest Edit" mode, the latter is 
extremely useful with multiple editor.  I am not sure if Drive, just as 
a file share, will version documents for you.  One aspect of Drive that 
doesn't work well at all is "Offline" mode.  I have often 'pinned' a 
document to my device before embarking on a long flight only to find, 
when I attempt to open the document, an error stating, "no valid 
internet connection."  Drives me crazy.

Anyway, my 2c.

Troy
