Hi!
Here's the program of a symposium soon to be held in Rome, at the Ecole 
française de Rome. It is organized jointly by French and Italian 
medievalists (from the Ecole française de Rome, LAMOP, and Reti 
Medievali), yet it is not restricted to the Middle Ages. Papers will be 
given in French and Italian.
If you want to attend, please contact Marilyn Nicoud or Grazia Parrino, 
_redacted_(a)efrome.it
Best wishes, Marjorie
