Subject: [dm-l] Digital Medievalist 11 (2018)Dear digital medievalists,I am pleased to announce the publication of Joris van Zundert's article 
"On Not Writing a Review about Mirador: Mirador, IIIF, and the 
Epistemological Gains of Distributed Digital Scholarly Resources" in the 
Journal of Digital Medievalist:
http://doi.org/10.16995/dm.78This article is one of four articles and three reviews published over 
the past few months on the new publication platform of Open Library in 
the Humanities (OLH).
