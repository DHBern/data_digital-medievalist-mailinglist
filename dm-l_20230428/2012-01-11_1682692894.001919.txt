For those of you in (or traveling to) France, I am very pleased to announce
the upcoming days of study linked to my "Edition électronique et sources
médiévales" seminar. The title, this year, is "L'édition électronique dans
tous ses états : évolution des pratiques, évolution des besoins".
All are welcome, students, researchers and professionals. Like last year, I
chose to have a smaller number of speakers, but each with a longer time, to
favour discussion and exchange.
Looking forward to meeting some of you there, maybe!
Marjorie
