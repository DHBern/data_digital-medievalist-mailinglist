Subject: [dm-l] 2020 DM Executive Board Election Results

Dear Digital Medievalist community,

We have the pleasure of announcing the results from the 2020 DM elections.

The tally for Digital Medievalist Executive Board Elections (term 2020-22) has been computed and the elected members of the community to the Board are (in alphabetical order):

  *   Luise Borek
  *   Tobias Hodel
  *   Laura Morreale
  *   Lynn Ransom
  *   Claudia Sojer

We would like to thank the other candidates for standing and providing us with an outstandingly rich choice. Thank you for your participation!

Best wishes to the new DM board, and the DM community as a whole,

Best,
Alberto, Jamie, and Georg

DM 2020 Election Committee
