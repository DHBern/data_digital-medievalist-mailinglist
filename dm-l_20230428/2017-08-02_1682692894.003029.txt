*The Italian Association of Digital Humanities (AIUCD) is pleased to=20
announce the 2018 edition of its annual conference and invites all=20
interested scholars to submit a proposal.**The theme of the conference is */Cultural Heritage in the Digital Age.=20
Memory, Humanities and Technologies/*. The topic brings together=20
interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary fields, and examines the role of=20
cultural heritage in all its aspects in the digital age, the way in=20
which the study and scientific research of humanistic knowledge=20
(literary, historical, artistic, archaeological and philosophical)=20
connects with different methodologies, logic and computer technologies=20
and various types of media and digital resources. It also concerns the=20
critical problems of digital memory, the re-thinking of contemporary=20
digital culture, the changing definition, enhancement, research and=20
conservation of cultural heritage in the digital era, as well as the=20
digital rebirth of humanistic culture.While open to other topics related to Digital Humanities, proposals for=20
contributions are particularly encouraged on the following:-Interaction and integration of methods of statistical/quantitative=20
analysis and formal logic methods in the Humanities.
-Semantic web technologies and Linked Open Data in the Humanities.
-Quantitative and stylometric analysis, topic modelling and clustering=20
for literary texts, archival documents and cultural objects.
-Authorship attribution and automatic text classification.
-Applications of, and experiments with, data mining methodologies in=20
historical, artistic and archaeological research.
-Network analysis and sentiment analysis applied to the Humanities.
-Interaction between Natural Language Processing technologies and data=20
mining in the Humanities.
-Methods and techniques of visualization and their impact on knowledge=20
transfer in Cultural Heritage and in the Humanities.
-Methodologies and applications of Natural Language and Text Processing=20
of corpora in the Humanities.
-Computational analysis of multimedia resources: images, audio and video.
-Sustainability problems and strategies for research data: persistence,=20
provenance and authenticity.
-Cultural and social impact of Humanities research produced with=20
computational methods.
-Big Data methods and technologies in Cultural Heritage and in the=20
Humanities.
-Internet of Things for Cultural Heritage and the Humanities.
-Landscape and long-term settlement: prospects and potential of digital=20
cartography.
-Public use, dissemination and didactic mediation of humanistic cultures=20
in the digital age.
-Digital narrative methods and techniques: story and place-telling.
-Methodologies and technologies of the digitization process for the=20
production and preservation of digital cultural heritage.
The *deadline* for submitting proposals is *September 30th (midnight)=20
2017*.
*Notifications of acceptance* will be sent to the authors *by October=20
30th 2017*.
The *official languages* of the Conference are *Italian and English*.=20
However, it is *possible to present in other languages if English slides=20
are provided*.Come to Bari!--=20
