Subject: [dm-l] Seals and Linked Open Data: December 2, 3 and 8

The Saint Louis University Center for Digital Humanities<https://www.slu.edu/arts-and-sciences/ong-center/index.php>, in association with Linked Past 6<https://ics.sas.ac.uk/events/event/22792>, is hosting a symposium on Seals and Linked Open Data<https://johnmcewan.github.io/LinkedPast_6/> (December 2,3 and 8, 2020). We will consider the future of sigillography by investigating the applications of new technologies, such as machine learning and 3D visualization, and by exploring the ongoing development of large sigillographic datasets for Medieval Europe. Please register here<https://www.eventbrite.com/e/seals-and-linked-open-data-tickets-129485222787> for our synchronous events.
