We would like to draw your attention to the Call for Papers for the conference Patristic Sermons in the Middle Ages: Collections, Mediators and the Practice of Compiling (full CfP available via https://applejack.science.ru.nl/passimproject/media/22.12.02_CfP_PASSIM_Conference2023.pdf)
