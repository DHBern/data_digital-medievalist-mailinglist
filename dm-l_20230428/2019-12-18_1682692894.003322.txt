*****Dear Colleagues,*Umanistica Digitale* (ISSN 2532-8816), the journal of the AIUCD (Italian
Association of Digital Humanities and Cultures), is pleased
to announce the publication of the 7th issue, available at
https://umanisticadigitale.unibo.it.This issue of the journal presents a selection of the papers presented at
the AIUCD 2018 conference (Bari, 31 January - 2  February 2018), whose main
topic was "Cultural Heritage in the Digital Age. Memory, Humanities and
Technologies."We warmly invite you to visit our web site anche check for articles of your
interest.
Regards and season's greetings,Fabio Ciotti--
